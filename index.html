About the Pack

The London Starter pack has everything you need for your first visit to London. We make it convenient for you to use public transport, call home, or find out what to expect in London.

Lebara SIM Card

A British SIM card, with highly competitive local rates and unbeatable international rates. Call your family or friends on arrival. Don't stress about international roaming charges.

Visitor Oyster Card

An exclusive Visitor Oyster card that gives you extensive access to Central London's public transit network, at the best price. The card provides touch-on, touch-off access for trains, underground, buses, ferries and the East London cable car, Emirates Skyway.

Guide Book

A fully illustrated guidebook on how to make your stay easy, leisurely and pleasurable. We provide tips on where to visit and what to see, while also helping you out with the little things, like how to recharge transit cards, how to hire bicycles, etc.
